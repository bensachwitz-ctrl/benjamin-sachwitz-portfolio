What I Do

Protecting operations, building relationships, and finding coverage others can't.

Commercial Trucking & Logging

Specialized coverage for fleets, owner-operators, and forestry contractors. I navigate the E&S market to find capacity where standard carriers won't write — from long-haul trucking to high-risk logging operations deep in the Lowcountry.

Risk Analysis & Strategy

Loss run analysis, risk profiling, and coverage gap identification. I translate complex exposures into clear strategies — helping clients understand their risks before they become claims, and structuring programs that actually fit their operations.

Client Relationships

Insurance is a people business. I build the kind of relationships where clients call me first — not because they have to, but because they trust the partnership. From first-time fleet owners to multi-generational operations, I make coverage personal.

Technology & Innovation

Bridging traditional insurance with modern tech. I leverage data analytics, digital workflows, and emerging tools to streamline the placement process — because the best risk professionals are also the most efficient ones.
