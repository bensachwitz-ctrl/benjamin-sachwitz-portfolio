Who I Am

Built for the Business
of Risk

I'm a Darla Moore School of Business graduate who found his calling at the intersection of relationships and risk. As an Account Manager at Swamp Fox Agency — the only endorsed agency of the SC Timber Producers Association — I specialize in the industries most people overlook: commercial trucking, logging operations, and the complex risks that keep South Carolina's economy moving.

My path wasn't traditional. Operations at The Masters, federal tech sales at Carahsoft in DC, specialty brokerage at Price Forbes inside Lloyd's of London — every stop built a lens that most 22-year-old account managers don't have. I bring a global perspective to a local market.

Every policy starts with a conversation, not a quote. The best risk strategies come from understanding the people behind the operations — their families, their crews, their livelihoods.

The Path

From Augusta to London and back. Scroll or use arrows.

Augusta, GA

The Masters Tournament

Operations · Seasonal

Elite event operations at the most prestigious tournament in golf.

Worked behind the scenes at Augusta National during the Masters — managing patron logistics, coordinating with operations leadership, and executing under a standard of excellence that accepts zero margin for error. Learned that every detail is a reflection of the standard you hold, and that operational discipline at this level translates directly into risk management. The precision required to run a world-class event is the same precision required to underwrite complex risks.

Washington, DC

Carahsoft Technology

Federal Tech Sales · Reston, VA

Enterprise cybersecurity & software sales into the federal government.

Sold cybersecurity and enterprise software solutions to federal agencies through complex government procurement cycles (GSA Schedule, GWACs, BPAs). Carahsoft is the #45 largest private company in America per Forbes — a pressure-cooker environment where you learn to build relationships at scale, translate technical capabilities into value propositions, and close deals with decision-makers who have zero patience for fluff. Every skill I use in insurance today started here.

Read more
London, UK

Price Forbes at Lloyd's

Global Specialty Broker · Lloyd's Market

Trained at the global epicenter of specialty insurance — Lloyd's of London.

Worked inside Price Forbes, a leading independent global specialty broker, on the floor of Lloyd's of London — the oldest and most sophisticated insurance marketplace in the world, established 1688. Gained first-hand exposure to how syndicates operate, how complex global risks are structured and placed, and how the London market underwrites exposures that no domestic carrier will touch. Understanding surplus lines at the source changed how I approach every placement back in the US.

Read more
Moncks Corner, SC

Swamp Fox Agency

Account Manager · 2024 - Present

Home. Commercial trucking and logging specialist. SCTPA-endorsed.

This is where every experience converges. Swamp Fox is the only endorsed agency of the SC Timber Producers Association and a member of the SC Trucking Association — founded 35+ years ago by logging equipment salesmen who saw an underserved market. I specialize in the commercial trucking and logging risks that are the backbone of the SC economy, working across the E&S market to find capacity where standard carriers won't write. Building a book on relationships, not transactions — with a global perspective applied to a local market. The agency also created FICoSE, the only logger-owned captive insurance company in the US.
