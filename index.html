About Me

Built on the World's
Toughest Markets.

I'm a Risk Management & Insurance graduate from the University of South Carolina's Darla Moore School of Business — finished December 2025, ahead of schedule. My foundation was built at Lloyd's of London through Price Forbes, where I supported Marine and Property underwriting teams across the full placement cycle.

At Swamp Fox Agency, I work in the E&S non-admitted market on some of the most complex and technically demanding risks in P&C — commercial trucking and logging across the Southeast. I analyze loss runs, interpret FMCSA and CAB fleet safety data, prepare Lloyd's binding authority submissions, and manage carrier negotiations using Applied Epic and Samsara telematics.

Before insurance, I spent three years in federal tech sales at Carahsoft and two seasons executing operations at The Masters Tournament at Augusta National. I'm also a builder — I develop AI-powered tools, digital platforms, and automation workflows to modernize how agencies operate.
