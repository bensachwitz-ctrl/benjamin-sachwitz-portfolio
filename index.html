About Me

Built on the World's
Toughest Markets.

Risk Management & Insurance graduate from the University of South Carolina's Darla Moore School of Business — finished December 2025, ahead of schedule. My foundation was built at Lloyd's of London through Price Forbes, where I supported Marine and Property underwriting teams across the full placement cycle.

At Swamp Fox Agency, I work in the E&S non-admitted market on some of the most complex risks in P&C — commercial trucking and logging across the Southeast. I analyze loss runs, interpret FMCSA and CAB fleet safety data, prepare Lloyd's binding authority submissions, and manage carrier negotiations using Applied Epic and Samsara telematics.

Before insurance, I spent two-plus years in federal tech sales at Carahsoft and two seasons on operations staff at The Masters Tournament at Augusta National. I also build AI-powered tools and digital platforms to modernize how agencies operate.
