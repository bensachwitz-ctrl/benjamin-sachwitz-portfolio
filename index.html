About

Global Pedigree.
Southern Roots.

I'm a Darla Moore School of Business graduate with a career arc that cuts across sport, federal technology, global insurance, and the frontlines of commercial risk — all before 23.

My time at Price Forbes (Lloyd's of London) gave me direct exposure to marine and property placement in the world's most sophisticated risk market. At Swamp Fox Agency, I specialize in commercial trucking and logging, applying a global underwriting lens to hard-to-place markets.
