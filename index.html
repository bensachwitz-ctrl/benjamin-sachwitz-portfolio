About

Built on the World's
Toughest Markets.

I'm a Risk Management & Insurance graduate from the University of South Carolina's Darla Moore School of Business — finishing December 2025, ahead of schedule. My foundation was built at Lloyd's of London through Price Forbes, where I supported Marine and Property underwriting teams, working through the full risk placement process from submission and broker negotiation to claims handling.

At Swamp Fox Agency, I work in the E&S non-admitted market on commercial trucking and logging risks — some of the most technically demanding classes in P&C. Day-to-day I analyze loss runs, interpret FMCSA and CAB reports, prepare submissions for Lloyd's binding authority markets, and support carrier negotiations using Applied Epic and Samsara telematics data.

Before insurance, I spent three years in federal tech sales at Carahsoft and two seasons executing operations at The Masters Tournament at Augusta National. That mix of global market exposure, government sales discipline, and high-pressure execution is what I bring to every account.
